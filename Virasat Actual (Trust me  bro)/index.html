Our Story

Preserving the Past,
Inspiring the Future.

Virasat '26 is more than just an event; it's a movement to revitalize our shared cultural identity. Born from a passion for history and art, Virasat has grown into a premier platform for artisans, performers, and historians.

This year, we focus on "Heritage in Harmony," exploring how ancient traditions seamlessly blend with modern expressions. Join us as we weave stories of resilience, beauty, and community.
